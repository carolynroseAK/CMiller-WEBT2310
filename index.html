Cyber Security Stuff

Cybersecurity Tips for Older Adults Stay safe, stay confident—your digital life matters. 1. Be skeptical of unexpected messages Don’t click on links or open attachments from unfamiliar senders. Even if a message looks like it’s from a friend or company, double-check—scammers often impersonate trusted sources.

2. Pause before acting on urgency Scammers use panic to pressure you. If someone says “act now” or “don’t tell anyone,” stop and talk to someone you trust.
